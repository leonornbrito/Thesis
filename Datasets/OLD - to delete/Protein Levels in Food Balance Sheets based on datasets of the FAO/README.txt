Protein Levels in Food Balance Sheets based on datasets of the FAO

Related publication:
A Worldwide Hotspot Analysis on Food Loss and Waste, Associated Greenhouse Gas Emissions, and Protein Losses
Guo, X., Broeze, J., Groot, J. J., Axmann, H. B. & Vollebregt, H. M., 2020, In: Sustainability. 12, 18, 7488.

Description:
This dataset contains data from the FAO Food Balance Sheets (1994 upto 2018) and columns are added containing the amount of protein

Keywords:
Food Balance Sheets
Food items
Protein

Spatial coverage:
World

Temporal coverage:
1994-2018

This dataset contains the following files:
20211214FoodBalanceSheets Protein1994-2013.csv
20211214FoodBalanceSheets Protein2014-2018.csv

Explanation of variables:
1994-2018 volume: an amount of a food item in tonnes
1994-2018 protein conten (kg): the amount of protein in the amount of the variable '1994-2018 Volume'
